You have entered the room of oddities. Rows and rows of porcelain dolls are staring blankly out at you. It is almost as if they can look into your soul and sense all the fear in your body. A sense of unease washes over you. You can't quite explain why, but something about the room seems off. Attempting to find a killer in the room that seems as if anything or anyone can attack you, is unnerving. Be quick to make sure you make it out alive!
